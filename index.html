About

Joey Montiel is a Chicana artist from Stockton, California. She started to draw at a young age and only developed her skills further as she got into high school. It was in high school that she started to have a finite style. Traditional art was her focus however, she started to experiment in the digital space. After a newfound passion for digital art, she applied to San Jose State University where she planned to pursue digital media art. She started to experiment with digital media and continues to do so to this day.
